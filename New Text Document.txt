🔬 From Raw Data to Real Insight: Exploring Nobel Prize Trends 🧠

I recently embarked on a data project that began on DataCamp using Python—and what started as a simple exercise quickly turned into a passion project.

Using the Nobel Prize dataset (1901–2023), I dug deep to uncover stories and patterns behind the world's most prestigious awards. But first, I had to roll up my sleeves:

The data wasn't clean, so I had to handle missing values

Correct inconsistent formatting

And remove irrelevant records that didn’t affect the analysis

Once the data was clean, I shifted to Power BI to bring the insights to life with visual storytelling.
